<h2><center>COVID-19</center></h2>
</font>
Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.
Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment.
<font color="green"> 
<h3>SYMPTOMS</h3>
</font>
<p>
<img src="C:\Users\balaji\Desktop\images.jpg"  hspace=300px  height=250px width=250px align="right">
Common symptoms:
<ul>
<li>fever.
<li>tiredness.
<li>dry cough.
</ul>
Some people may experience:
<ul>
<li>aches and pains.
<li>nasal congestion.
<li>runny nose.
<li>sore throat.
<li>diarrhoea.
</ul>
On average it takes 5–6 days from when someone is infected with the virus for symptoms to show, however it can take up to 14 days.
People with mild symptoms who are otherwise healthy should self-isolate. Seek medical attention if you have a fever, a cough, and difficulty breathing.
</p>
<img src="C:\Users\balaji\Desktop\prevention.jpg"  hspace=100px  height=400px width=400px align="right">
<font color="green"> 
<h3>PREVENTIONS</h3>
</font>
<p>
<img
To prevent the spread of COVID-19:
<ul>
<li>Clean your hands often. 
<li>Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
<li>Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
<li>Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.
<li>Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
<li>Stay home if you feel unwell.
<li>If you have a fever, a cough, and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention.
<li>Follow the directions of your local health authority.
</ul>
</p>
